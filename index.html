Signal File // ARCEOLOGY

Arceology is an ARC Raiders content creator documenting the emotional reality of survival in Speranza. Every raid, every extraction, every loss gets recorded—not as strategy guides or tier lists, but as honest, story-driven field logs that capture what it feels like to be human in a world that rewards numbness.

This site is a living archive of those experiences. The field logs are personal journals written in-character from the perspective of a raider who refuses to stop feeling, even when survival demands it. The gameplay footage is raw helmet cam recordings that pair with each written entry. The gear is ARC Raiders–inspired apparel designed to carry the aesthetic of Speranza into the real world.

Every piece of content here is independently created by Arceology. This is not an official ARC Raiders property—it is a fan-driven creative project born from a deep connection to the game, its world, and the stories it makes possible.
