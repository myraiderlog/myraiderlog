Raider FAQ

common questions // field answers
General

What is ARC Raiders?

+

ARC Raiders is a third-person cooperative extraction shooter developed by Embark Studios. Players take on the role of Raiders fighting against ARC machines in a post-apocalyptic world set in a place called Speranza. It launched in Early Access on Steam in October 2025 and has since grown into one of the top extraction shooters on the platform.

Is ARC Raiders free to play?

+

No. ARC Raiders launched as a paid Early Access title at $39.99 for the standard edition and $59.99 for the Deluxe edition. Embark moved away from their original free-to-play model before launch. The game has sold over 12.4 million copies as of early 2026.

Can you play ARC Raiders solo?

+

Yes. The Headwinds update (January 2026) added Solo vs Squads matchmaking for level 40+ Raiders. Solo players get extra XP for entering squad lobbies alone. The game is viable solo, but squad play is recommended for harder content. Check our solo build guide for optimized loadouts.

What platforms is ARC Raiders on?

+

ARC Raiders is currently available on PC via Steam Early Access. Console versions have not been announced yet but Embark has stated they are exploring other platforms.

Gameplay & Mechanics

What is Expedition in ARC Raiders?

+

Expedition is the seasonal character reset system. When you depart on an Expedition, your level, stash, inventory, loadouts, and skill tree reset. In exchange, you earn permanent rewards including Skill Points (up to 5 for 3M stash value), stash space (12-24 slots), cosmetics, and stacking buffs for consecutive departures. It is optional but highly recommended. See our Expedition prep guide for details.

How do Merits work in ARC Raiders?

+

Merits are earned by destroying, assisting, and damaging ARC machines. During events like Shared Watch, Merits are the primary progression currency with no PvP Merits awarded. Merit thresholds unlock tiered event rewards including exclusive cosmetics and event-specific loot. Check the intel board for current event details.

How does extraction work?

+

Extraction is how you keep the loot you find topside. You need to reach an extraction point and successfully call for extraction, which alerts nearby players and ARC machines. If you die before extracting, you lose everything you carried in. The key is knowing extraction point locations, timing your runs, and reading the map for threats. Our extraction zone guide covers every extraction point.

What are map conditions?

+

Map conditions are environmental modifiers that change how maps play. Cold Snap adds frostbite damage when you stay exposed, Shrouded Sky introduces gusty wind that affects visibility and movement, and more are coming with Flashpoint (lightning hazards) and Riven Tides. Each condition changes loot tables and enemy behavior.

What is Stella Montis?

+

Stella Montis is one of the largest and most dangerous ARC enemy types in the game. It acts as a boss-level encounter requiring coordinated squad tactics to defeat. Stella Montis drops high-value loot and is a key target for the Trophy Display system. See our boss guide for strategies.

Escalation Season

What is the Escalation season roadmap?

+

The Escalation season runs January through April 2026 with four major content drops: Headwinds (January) brought Solo vs Squads and Trophy Display, Shrouded Sky (February) adds wind conditions and a new ARC enemy, Flashpoint (March) brings lightning hazards and Scrappy improvements, and Riven Tides (April) features a new map and a major ARC boss. Track all updates on our operations board.

What is the Raider Deck?

+

The Raider Deck is a progression track released with each major update during the Escalation season. It includes gadgets, cosmetics, and Raider Tokens as rewards. A new Raider Deck is expected with each monthly update.

Glossary
ARC
The hostile machine faction that players fight against. Origin and full meaning remain mysterious.
Speranza
The underground settlement where Raiders live between topside runs. Acts as the game hub.
Topside
The surface world above Speranza where extraction missions take place.
Extraction
The act of leaving a mission with your loot intact. Die before extracting and you lose it all.
Expedition
Seasonal character reset that grants permanent rewards like Skill Points and stash space.
Merits
Points earned from fighting ARC machines, used during events for reward progression.
Coins
Primary in-game currency. Stash value in Coins determines Expedition rewards.
Scrappy
A companion robot that aids Raiders. Getting improvements in the Flashpoint update.
Trophy Display
A player project for hunting and mounting ARC trophies, introduced in Headwinds.
Rust Belt
The geographic region where ARC Raiders takes place.
Raider Deck
Seasonal progression track with cosmetics, gadgets, and Raider Tokens.
Candleberries
Collectible items found during Cold Snap conditions. No longer event-tied.
