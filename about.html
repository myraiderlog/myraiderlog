Who Is Arceology?

Arceology is an independent ARC Raiders content creator building a living archive of survival stories, gameplay footage, and raider-inspired gear from the world of Speranza. Everything on this site is created by one person with one belief: that the best gaming content comes from honesty, not performance.

Most ARC Raiders content focuses on optimal loadouts, extraction strategies, and competitive advantage. This project takes a different approach. The field logs published here are story-driven journal entries written from the perspective of a raider who refuses to stop feeling—even when survival demands emotional detachment. Each entry is tied to real in-game locations, events, and the moral weight of decisions made under pressure.

"I wear my emotions on my sleeve. I can't help it."

What This Site Is

MyRaiderLog.com is a fan-created content hub dedicated to ARC Raiders. It is not an official Embark Studios property, and it is not affiliated with the ARC Raiders development team. This is a creative project built on a genuine connection to the game and its world.

The site serves as a central archive for all of Arceology's ARC Raiders content. The field logs section contains narrative journal entries that document each raid with emotional depth and storytelling. The gameplay section features raw helmet cam footage from Speranza—unedited runs that pair directly with the written entries. The gear section showcases ARC Raiders-inspired apparel and accessories designed by Arceology and available through Bonfire.

This project is supported through Ko-fi contributions and merchandise sales. Every piece of support helps keep the archive running and funds the creation of new content.

The Raider Log Concept

A raider log is a personal field journal. In the world of ARC Raiders, raiders go topside to scavenge resources from ARC-controlled territory on Speranza. Each run is dangerous. Each extraction is uncertain. The raider logs capture what happens between the bullets—the quiet moments, the moral dilemmas, the cost of choosing survival over compassion.

Each log entry follows a consistent format: a day number, a date in the Speranza calendar, an in-game location, and a first-person account of what happened during the run. Some entries document successful extractions. Others document failure. All of them document the emotional reality of existing in a world where empathy is a liability.

The raider logs are fiction rooted in real gameplay. Every location mentioned is a real ARC Raiders map location. Every scenario described is something that happened during an actual run. The stories are dramatized, but the feelings are real.

Content Philosophy

Arceology creates content that prioritizes emotional authenticity over algorithm optimization. The field logs are written to be read slowly. The gameplay footage is presented without flashy editing or clickbait commentary. The gear is designed to feel like something a real raider would wear—worn-in, tactical, functional.

This approach means the content may not appeal to everyone. It is not designed to. It is designed for the kind of gamer who finishes a raid and sits quietly for a moment thinking about what just happened. For the player who names their loadout. For the one who feels something when another raider goes down and there is nothing they can do about it.

If that sounds like you, welcome to the underground.
